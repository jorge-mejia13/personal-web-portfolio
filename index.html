About

Hello there

Hi, I'm Jorge Mejia. Web Application Developer

I'm a passionate Web Application Development student at Ranken Technical College with a strong foundation in front-end and back-end technologies. My journey into software development began when I wanted to know how websites were made and how websites work, and has evolved into a deep commitment to creating clean, efficient, and user-friendly web applications. The languages and frameworks I have learned at school are HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and C#.

With hands-on experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, C#, and modern frameworks like Bootstrap, I enjoy transforming complex problems into elegant solutions. I'm particularly interested in creating seamless user experiences. When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies. I believe in continuous learning and staying current with industry trends. My goal is to become a full-stack developer who creates impactful applications that make a difference in people's lives.
